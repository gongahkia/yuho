## GA

Statute text: "shall be punished with imprisonment for a term of not less than 2 years and not more than 5 years and with caning."

Current Yuho grammar requires `caning := <n> strokes` with a numeric count or range. Section 385 mandates caning but does not specify any number of strokes, so the caning limb is preserved verbatim in `supplementary` rather than fabricating a stroke count.
