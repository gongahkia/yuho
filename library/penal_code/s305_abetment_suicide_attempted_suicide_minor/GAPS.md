## GA

Quoted statute text:

> shall be punished with death or imprisonment for life, or with imprisonment for a term which may extend to 20 years

and

> shall be punished with imprisonment for life or with imprisonment for a term which may extend to 20 years

Current Yuho grammar does not provide a structured `imprisonment := life` clause. The life-imprisonment limbs for section 305(1)(a) and section 305(1)(c) are therefore preserved verbatim in `supplementary` text rather than fabricated into a non-existent structured penalty form.
