## GA

Statute text: "shall be punished with imprisonment for life, or with imprisonment for a term which may extend to 15 years"

Current Yuho grammar does not provide a structured `imprisonment := life` clause. Section 326 therefore preserves the life-imprisonment limb verbatim in `supplementary` text instead of fabricating a non-existent structured penalty form.

## GB

Statute text: "shall also be liable to caning"

The current Yuho grammar requires `caning` to be encoded with a numeric stroke count or range. Section 326 states liability to caning without specifying any minimum or maximum number of strokes, so the caning limb is preserved verbatim in `supplementary` rather than fabricated as a numeric `caning := ... strokes` clause.
