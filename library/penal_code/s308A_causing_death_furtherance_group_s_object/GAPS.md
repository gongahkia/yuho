## GA
"A person who is guilty of an offence under subsection (1) shall be punished with imprisonment for life or imprisonment for a term which may extend to 20 years, and shall also be liable to caning. [15/2019]"

The current grammar has no direct way to encode liability to caning where the statute gives no stroke count or range. Encoding a numeric range would fabricate a fact, so the caning limb is preserved in `supplementary` text instead.
