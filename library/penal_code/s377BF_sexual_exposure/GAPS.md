## GA
"A person who commits an offence under subsection (1) or (2) against a person ( B ) who is below 14 years of age shall on conviction be punished with imprisonment for a term which may extend to 2 years, and shall also be liable to fine or to caning. [15/2019]"

The grammar can encode the imprisonment limb and the uncapped fine limb, but it cannot encode liability "to caning" where the statute gives no stroke count or range. The encoding therefore preserves the caning limb in `supplementary` text rather than inventing a number.
