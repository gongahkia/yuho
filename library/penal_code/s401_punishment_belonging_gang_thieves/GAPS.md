## GA

Statute text: "shall also be punished with caning with not less than 4 strokes. [15/2019]"

The current Yuho grammar supports `caning := <n> strokes` and `caning := <min> .. <max> strokes`, but it does not support a minimum-only caning clause with no stated upper bound. This encoding preserves the caning requirement verbatim in `supplementary` and does not invent a maximum.
