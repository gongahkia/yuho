## GA

Statute text: "shall be punished with imprisonment for a term which may extend to 7 years, and shall also be liable to fine or to caning."

Gap: the current grammar supports `fine := unlimited` for an uncapped fine, but `caning` requires an explicit numeric stroke count. Section 330 states liability to caning without specifying any number of strokes, so the caning limb is preserved in `supplementary` text without inventing a stroke range.
