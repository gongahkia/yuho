## GA
"shall, if the offence is committed, be punished with imprisonment for a term which may extend to one-fourth, and if the offence is not committed, to one-eighth of the longest term provided for that offence"

The current grammar accepts fixed `imprisonment := ...` durations and `penalty when ...` branches, but it does not express a proportional imprisonment term derived from another offence's maximum sentence.

## GB
"or with such fine as is provided for the offence, or with both"

The current grammar accepts fixed money ranges and `fine := unlimited`, but it does not express a fine amount that is dynamically inherited from the predicate offence's own punishment provision.
