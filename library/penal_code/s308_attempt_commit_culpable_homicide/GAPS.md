## GA

Statute text: "and if hurt is caused to any person by such act, the offender shall be punished with imprisonment for a term which may extend to 15 years, or with fine, or with caning, or with any combination of such punishments. [15/2019]"

Yuho currently requires a numeric `caning := <n> strokes` or range, but section 308 states liability to caning without an express stroke count. This encoding preserves the caning limb verbatim in `supplementary` text for the aggravated branch and does not invent a stroke count.
