## GA

Statute text: "(3) A person who is guilty of an offence under subsection (1) or (2) shall on conviction be punished with imprisonment for a term which may extend to 5 years, and shall also be liable to fine or to caning."

The current grammar can encode the imprisonment limb and the uncapped fine limb, but it cannot encode a caning liability where the statute provides no numeric stroke count or range without fabricating one.
