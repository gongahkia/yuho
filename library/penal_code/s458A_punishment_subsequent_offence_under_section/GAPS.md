## GA
Statute text: "Whoever, having been convicted of an offence under section 449, 450, 451 or 452, commits an offence under section 449, 450, 451 or 452 shall be liable to caning in addition to the punishment prescribed for that offence. [15/2019]"

The current Yuho grammar has no direct way to encode liability to caning where the statute gives no stroke count or range. Encoding a numeric range would fabricate a fact, so the caning limb is preserved in `supplementary` text instead.
