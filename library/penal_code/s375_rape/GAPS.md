## GA

Statute text: "Subject to subsection (3), a man who is guilty of an offence under this section shall be punished with imprisonment for a term which may extend to 20 years, and shall also be liable to fine or to caning."

Gap: The grammar can encode `fine := unlimited` and `or_both`, but it cannot encode liability to caning where the statute specifies no stroke count or range. The caning exposure is therefore preserved in `supplementary` text.

## GB

Statute text: "shall be punished with imprisonment for a term of not less than 8 years and not more than 20 years and shall also be punished with caning of not less than 12 strokes."

Gap: The grammar can encode the imprisonment minimum and range, but it cannot encode caning with a statutory minimum and no express maximum. The caning minimum is therefore preserved in `supplementary` text.
