SECTION 463: Forgery
============================================================

Definitions:
  "forgery" means Making a false document with fraudulent intent
  "false_document" means A document that dishonestly or fraudulently purports to be something it is not
  "making" means Creating, altering, or signing a document falsely
  "valuable_security" means Document purporting to create, transfer, or release any right

Elements of the offence:
  Physical element (actus reus): Making a false document or part of document
  Mental element (mens rea): Intent to cause damage or injury to public or person
  Mental element (mens rea): Intent to support any claim or title
  Mental element (mens rea): Intent to cause person to part with property
  Mental element (mens rea): Intent to cause person to enter contract
  Mental element (mens rea): Intent to commit fraud

Penalty:
  Shall be punished with imprisonment for a term of not less than no time and not more than 4 years, or with a fine of not less than S$0.00 and not more than S$10,000.00, or with any combination thereof.

Illustrations:

example1 A creates a false certificate purporting to be from a university. A has committed forgery.
example2 A alters the date on a document to make it appear earlier than it was. A has committed forgery.
example3 A signs B's name on a cheque without B's authority. A has committed forgery.
