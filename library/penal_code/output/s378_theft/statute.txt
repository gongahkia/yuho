SECTION 378: Theft
============================================================

Definitions:
  "theft" means Dishonestly taking movable property out of possession of another without consent
  "movable_property" means Corporeal property except land and things attached to earth
  "dishonestly" means With intention of causing wrongful gain or wrongful loss
  "wrongful_gain" means Gain by unlawful means of property to which the gainer is not legally entitled
  "wrongful_loss" means Loss by unlawful means of property to which the loser is legally entitled

Elements of the offence:
  Mental element (mens rea): Dishonest intention to take property
  Physical element (actus reus): Taking movable property
  Circumstance: Property in possession of another person
  Circumstance: Without consent of that person
  Physical element (actus reus): Moving the property in order to such taking

Penalty:
  Shall be punished with imprisonment for a term of not less than no time and not more than 7 years, or with a fine of not less than S$0.00 and not more than S$10,000.00, or with any combination thereof.

Illustrations:

example1 A finds a watch on the road, not knowing to whom it belongs. A picks it up with intent to find the owner. A has not committed theft.
example2 A finds a watch belonging to B. A picks it up intending to keep it. A has committed theft.
example3 A sees a watch belonging to B lying on a table. A, not intending to take the watch, moves it to see what is under it. A has not committed theft.
