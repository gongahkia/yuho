SECTION 383: Extortion
============================================================

Definitions:
  "extortion" means Intentionally putting any person in fear of injury to induce property delivery
  "injury" means Harm illegally caused to any person in body, mind, reputation, or property
  "fear" means Apprehension of injury to oneself or another
  "property" means Any movable or immovable property

Elements of the offence:
  Mental element (mens rea): Intentionally putting a person in fear
  Physical element (actus reus): Putting person in fear of injury
  Circumstance: Fear of injury to person or another
  Physical element (actus reus): Inducing delivery of property or valuable security

Penalty:
  Shall be punished with imprisonment for a term of not less than 2 years and not more than 7 years, or with a fine of not less than S$0.00 and not more than S$10,000.00, or with any combination thereof.

  Additionally: May be liable to caning

Illustrations:

example1 A threatens to publish a defamatory statement about B unless B gives A money. A commits extortion.
example2 A threatens to harm B's child unless B hands over property. A commits extortion.
