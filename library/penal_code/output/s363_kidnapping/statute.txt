SECTION 363: Punishment for Kidnapping
============================================================

Definitions:
  "kidnapping_from_singapore" means Conveying any person beyond the limits of Singapore without the consent of that person
  "kidnapping_from_lawful_guardianship" means Taking or enticing any minor under 14, or any person of unsound mind, out of the keeping of the lawful guardian without consent

Elements of the offence:
  Mental element (mens rea): Intention to kidnap the person
  Physical element (actus reus): Conveying person beyond Singapore or taking from lawful guardianship
  Circumstance: Without the consent of the person or lawful guardian

Penalty:
  Shall be punished with imprisonment for a term of not less than no time and not more than 10 years, or with a fine of not less than S$0.00 and not more than S$30,000.00, or with caning with not less than 0 and not more than 12 strokes, or with any combination thereof.

Illustrations:

example1 A forcibly takes B, a child of 10, from B's parents and transports B to Malaysia. A has committed kidnapping.
