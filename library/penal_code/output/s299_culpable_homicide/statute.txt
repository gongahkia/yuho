SECTION 299: Culpable Homicide
============================================================

Definitions:
  "culpable_homicide" means Causing death by an act done with intention or knowledge
  "death" means The cessation of life
  "bodily_injury" means Physical harm to the body likely to cause death
  "act" means Any voluntary physical movement

Elements of the offence:
  Physical element (actus reus): Doing an act that causes death
  Physical element (actus reus): Omission that causes death
  Circumstance: Death of the victim is caused
  Mental element (mens rea): Intention of causing death
  Mental element (mens rea): Intention of causing bodily injury likely to cause death
  Mental element (mens rea): Knowledge that the act is likely to cause death

Penalty:
  Shall be punished with imprisonment for a term of not less than 5 years and not more than 20 years, or with a fine of not less than S$0.00 and not more than S$50,000.00, or with any combination thereof.

  Additionally: May be liable to caning

Illustrations:

example1 A lays sticks and turf over a pit, with the intention of causing death. B walks over and falls in and is killed. A has committed culpable homicide.
example2 A knows that B is behind a bush. C does not know it. A induces C to fire at the bush. C fires and kills B. A has committed culpable homicide.
