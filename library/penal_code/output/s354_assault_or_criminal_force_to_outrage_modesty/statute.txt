SECTION 354: Assault or Criminal Force to Outrage Modesty
============================================================

Definitions:
  "outrage_modesty" means Using criminal force or assault on any person with intent to or knowing it likely to outrage the modesty of that person
  "criminal_force" means Intentionally using force on any person without that person's consent
  "assault" means Making any gesture or preparation intending or knowing it to be likely to cause apprehension of criminal force

Elements of the offence:
  Mental element (mens rea): Intent to outrage modesty or knowledge that act is likely to outrage modesty
  Physical element (actus reus): Assault or use of criminal force on any person
  Circumstance: Act outrages the modesty of the person

Penalty:
  Shall be punished with imprisonment for a term of not less than no time and not more than 5 years, or with a fine of not less than S$0.00 and not more than S$10,000.00, or with caning with not less than 0 and not more than 6 strokes, or with any combination thereof.

Illustrations:

example1 A grabs B's arm and touches B inappropriately without consent. A intends to outrage B's modesty. A is guilty under section 354.
example2 A, in a crowded place, deliberately presses against B knowing it is likely to outrage B's modesty. A is guilty under section 354.
