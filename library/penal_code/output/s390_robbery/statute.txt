SECTION 390: Robbery
============================================================

Definitions:
  "robbery" means Theft or extortion accompanied by violence or fear of violence
  "theft" means Taking property dishonestly without consent
  "extortion" means Inducing delivery of property through fear
  "hurt" means Causing bodily pain, disease, or infirmity
  "wrongful_restraint" means Voluntarily obstructing a person to prevent movement

Elements of the offence:
  Physical element (actus reus): Committing theft
  Physical element (actus reus): Committing extortion
  Circumstance: Causing or attempting to cause death, hurt, or wrongful restraint
  Circumstance: Fear of instant death, hurt, or wrongful restraint

Penalty:
  Shall be punished with imprisonment for a term of not less than 3 years and not more than 14 years.

  Additionally: Shall be liable to caning with not less than 12 strokes

Illustrations:

example1 A holds B and takes B's wallet while B is unable to resist. A commits robbery.
example2 A threatens B with a knife and demands B's money. B, fearing for his life, hands over the money. A commits robbery.
