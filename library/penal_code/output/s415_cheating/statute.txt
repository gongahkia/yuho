SECTION 415: Cheating
============================================================

Definitions:
  "deceive" means To cause a person to believe something that is false
  "fraudulently" means With intent to defraud another person
  "dishonestly" means With intention of causing wrongful gain or wrongful loss
  "property" means Any movable or immovable property
  "inducement" means Causing a person to act based on deception

Elements of the offence:
  ALL of the following:
    Physical element (actus reus): Deceiving any person
    ANY of the following:
      Mental element (mens rea): Fraudulently inducing the person
      Mental element (mens rea): Dishonestly inducing the person
    Physical element (actus reus): Inducing delivery of property, consent to retain property, or act/omission
    Circumstance: Causing or likely to cause damage to body, mind, reputation, or property

Penalty:
  Shall be punished with imprisonment for a term of not less than 1 year and not more than 7 years, or with a fine of not less than S$0.00 and not more than S$50,000.00, or with any combination thereof.

Illustrations:

example1 A intentionally deceives B into believing that a worthless article is valuable, and thus induces B to buy it. A cheats.
example2 A falsely pretends to be in government service and induces B to let him have goods on credit. A cheats.
example3 A deceives B into believing that A means to repay a loan, inducing B to lend money which A does not intend to repay. A cheats.
