SECTION 319: Hurt
============================================================

Definitions:
  "hurt" means Causing bodily pain, disease, infirmity, or unconsciousness
  "bodily_pain" means Physical pain to any part of the body
  "disease" means A disorder of structure or function in a human body
  "infirmity" means Physical or mental weakness
  "unconsciousness" means State of being unaware of surroundings

Elements of the offence:
  Physical element (actus reus): Causing hurt to any person
  Circumstance: Bodily pain caused to victim
  Circumstance: Disease caused to victim
  Circumstance: Infirmity caused to victim
  Circumstance: Unconsciousness caused to victim

Penalty:
  Shall be punished with imprisonment for a term of not less than no time and not more than 2 years, or with a fine of not less than S$0.00 and not more than S$5,000.00, or with any combination thereof.

Illustrations:

example1 A strikes B with a stick. B feels pain. A has caused hurt to B.
example2 A intentionally administers a substance to B that causes B to become unconscious. A has caused hurt to B.
