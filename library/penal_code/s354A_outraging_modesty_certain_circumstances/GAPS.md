## GA

Statute text: "shall be punished with imprisonment for a term of not less than 2 years and not more than 10 years and with caning."

The current Yuho grammar requires a numeric stroke count for `caning := ... strokes`. Section 354A mandates caning in subsections (1) and (2) without specifying any minimum or maximum number of strokes, so the caning limb is preserved verbatim in `supplementary` rather than fabricated as a numeric caning clause.
