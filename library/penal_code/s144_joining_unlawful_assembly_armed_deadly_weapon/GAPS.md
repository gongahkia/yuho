## GA

Statute text: "shall be punished with imprisonment for a term which may extend to 5 years, or with fine, or with caning, or with any combination of such punishments."

The current Yuho grammar requires `caning := <n> strokes` with a numeric count or range. Section 144 imposes caning without specifying any number of strokes, so the caning limb is preserved verbatim in `supplementary` rather than fabricated as a numeric `caning := ... strokes` clause.
