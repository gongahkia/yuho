## GA

Quoted statute text: "shall also be liable to fine or to caning. [15/2019]"

`statute.yh` encodes `fine := unlimited`, but Yuho's current `caning` clause requires a numeric stroke count or range. Section 512(1) creates liability to caning without specifying a stroke count, so the caning limb is preserved in the subsection (1) `supplementary` text rather than a `caning := ... strokes` clause to avoid inventing a number.
