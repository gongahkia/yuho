## GA

Statute text: "Whoever commits piracy shall be punished with imprisonment for life ..."

Current Yuho grammar does not provide an `imprisonment := life` form, so the life-imprisonment term is preserved in `supplementary` text instead of a structured imprisonment clause.

## GB

Statute text: "... and with caning with not less than 12 strokes ..."

Current Yuho grammar does not provide a minimum-only caning form. Encoding `caning := 12 strokes` would understate the statute, and encoding a range would invent an upper bound, so the minimum caning term is preserved in `supplementary` text instead.
