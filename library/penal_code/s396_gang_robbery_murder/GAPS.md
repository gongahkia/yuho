## GA

Statute text: "and if he is not sentenced to death, shall also be punished with caning with not less than 12 strokes."

The current Yuho grammar supports `caning := <n> strokes` and `caning := <min> .. <max> strokes`, but it does not support a minimum-only caning clause with no stated upper bound. This encoding preserves the caning requirement in `supplementary` text and does not invent a maximum.
